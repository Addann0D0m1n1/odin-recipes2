Cereal

A hearty grain breakfast.

Pour a bowl of your favorite cereal for a quickstart to you day. Just add your favorite milk and some diced fruit and you're good to go! Grab life by the bowls.

If that's not enough, try adding honey and nuts, and maybe even some powdered sugar. But don't go too crazy! Heart disease is reaching epidemic levels!

Ingredients

  • Granola Cereal
  • Milk
  • Fresh Fruit
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Process

  1. Fetch Bowl
  2. Pour Cereal
  3. Add Fresh Fruit
  4. Add Milk
  5. Enjoy!
